ABOUT US

Founded in 2020, Stomach Klinic specializes in the best local and foreign comfort food there is. We pride ourselves in being sustainable, and environmentally friendly. Every burger, salad and sandwich we make starts by working closely with local farmers & partners. We take as much pride in preparing and cooking our food as we do in getting to know the farmers who grow it. We cook fresh, and we serve fresh. So whether you are health-conscious, environmentally conscious, or simply love great food, we’ve got something on our menu that will make you smile.

MISSION

To pride ourselves to provide sustainable, environmentally consicous, and fresh food to everyone, at an affordable price. We want to lower the amount of people who go hungry. Any food leftover instead of going to the trash, we donate it to homeless shelters, and food pantries.

OUR HISTORY

Stomach klinic is a restaurant and catering outfit, dedicated to serving you the best of tasteful and appetizing traditional and intercontinental delicacies you won’t be able to resist. Our menu includes dishes that are both healthy and pleasurable to the senses. Our unique style of cooking makes us stand out and takes you on a trip of appetizing flavours which will leave you coming back for more. We see our customers as royalty and we are committed to providing them with the best that they deserve.
